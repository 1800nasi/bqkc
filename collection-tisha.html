Tisha was also instrumental in managing Soakie’s: Kansas City’s former Black gay bar in Downtown Kansas City. The Tisha Taylor Collection, an inaugural collection donated to {B/qKC}, consists of various photos from Taylor’s time at Soakie’s and Kansas City’s Black Pride in 1999. " ---

Tisha Taylor was a beloved performer and community builder in Kansas City’s Black queer history. This collection gathers images, media, and ephemera related to her life and legacy.
